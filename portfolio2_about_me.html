About Me

I’m Josien Vekemans, 25 years old. I’m an ex-English teacher who didn’t see eye to eye with the Dutch education system so after some trial and error, I decided to learn a different trade.

I’m from Tilburg but I currently live in Leiden and work in Amsterdam. When I’m not working, reading, or writing I spend my weekends as a medieval reenactor.
